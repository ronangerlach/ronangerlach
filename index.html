About

Curious about how data and strategy shape real decisions

I'm a rising junior at Riverdale Country School in New York City, drawn to the intersection of economics, data, and strategy. I'm especially curious about fintech and how emerging technology is reshaping traditional finance- from payments to everyday banking. I thrive in collaborative settings where I can apply data and strategic thinking to real-world problems, and I'm always looking for the next case study, competition, or conversation that sharpens that thinking and challenges me.

Outside of the classroom, I enjoy traveling, fishing, and spending time with family and friends.

Background & Activities

Where I've put these skills to work

I got an early look at the finance industry interning at Novo, a neobank, where I analyzed and pitched strategies to strengthen the company's marketing and SEO approach. That experience aligned with a business pitch I co-developed with a teammate, which earned runner-up honors and was the highest-ranking underclassman proposal in my school's competition. I also spend time on things I love: I play varsity baseball, ski expertly, perform on trumpet, and have studied Mandarin Chinese for roughly a decade. Over the past two summers, I've supplemented my coursework with programs at Boston University and Wharton.
